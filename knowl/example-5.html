Example 1.1.7.

Consider the equation \(u^3+27w=0\text{.}\) We want to solve this equation for \(u\text{.}\)
\(u^3+27w\) \(=\) \(0\) The given equation
\(u^3+27w \color{red}{-27w}\) \(=\) \(0 \color{red}{-27w}\) subtract \(27w\) from both sides
\(u^3\) \(=\) \(-27w\) simplify
\(u\) \(=\) \(\sqrt[3]{-27w}\) take cube root, without \(\pm \) sign
\(u\) \(=\) \(-3\sqrt[3]{w}\) simplify
This time we found only one solution, \(u=-3\sqrt[3]{w}\text{.}\)
